Introduction

We collect certain personal information from our users to provide and improve our services. The types of information we collect include:
1.1. Name, to identify and address you appropriately;
1.2. Email Address, for communication purposes, including responses to inquiries and updates about our website; and
1.3. Phone Number.
However, providing phone number is optional and can be provided for additional contact options.
This information is collected when you fill out and submit the form for contacting us on our website. We only collect the information you provide that typically includes your name, email address, and optionally your phone number. We do not use cookies, tracking technologies, or third-party services to collect information. All information is provided directly by you through our website form. Further, we do not collect any sensitive information such as health data, financial information, or any other special categories of personal data.

Use Of Information And Legal Basis For Processing

We use the personal information we collect to provide you with the best possible support and to manage our interactions with you. This includes using your name and email address to respond to your inquiries, provide requested information, and communicate with you about your needs. If you provide your phone number, we may also contact you by phone if necessary to address your request or provide support. Additionally, we use your data to tailor our assistance to your needs and to ensure efficient handling of your requests. We may also process your personal data to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests, including fulfilling our obligations under data protection laws such as GDPR, CCPA, and DPDPA.
We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:
2.1. By filling out and submitting form on our website, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us, though this will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
2.2. We may process your information when it is necessary for the performance of a contract to which you are a party, or to take steps at your request before entering into such a contract.
2.3. We may process your personal data to comply with our legal obligations, such as maintaining accurate records for regulatory compliance and responding to lawful requests from public authorities.
2.4. We may process your information based on our legitimate interests, provided that these interests are not overridden by your rights and interests. This includes improving our website, interactions, and user experience.
We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. When we no longer need to use your personal information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

Collection And Storage Of Data By Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is kept confidential and is only accessible to the founders and the developer of LegalPalz. This access is strictly controlled to ensure the security and privacy of your information. We use your personal information solely for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy and do not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your data to external parties.

Security Measures

We take the security of your personal information seriously. To protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, we implement the following security measures:
4.1. Access Control: Only the founders and the developer have access to the personal information collected through our website. This access is tightly controlled to ensure the highest level of security.
4.2. Data Storage: Your personal information is stored securely in an Excel sheet, which is only accessible by authorized personnel.
Despite these measures, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

User rights

As a user of LegalPalz, you have certain rights regarding your personal information under applicable data protection laws, including GDPR, CCPA, and DPDPA. These rights include:
5.1. The right to request access to the personal information we hold about you.
5.2. The right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
5.3. The right to request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions. Exceptions may include situations where we need to retain your information to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, or for other legitimate business purposes.
5.4. The right to object to or request the restriction of processing your personal information under certain circumstances. This includes situations where you contest the accuracy of the data, the processing is unlawful but you oppose the deletion of the data, we no longer need the data but you require it for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims, or you have objected to processing and verification of whether our legitimate grounds override your rights is pending.
5.5. The right to request the transfer of your personal information to another service provider, where technically feasible.
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the email address provided in the "Contact Information" section of our website. We will respond to your request within the timeframes required by law and may ask for verification of your identity to ensure that your rights are respected.

Cookies and tracking technologies

We do not use any cookies or tracking technologies on our website. This means we do not collect any information through cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies. Your browsing activity on our website is not tracked, and no personal information is collected automatically.

Third-party links

Our website does not contain links to third-party websites or services. We are solely responsible for the content and practices of legalpalz.com. Should we decide to include any third-party links in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and inform you of any changes.

Compliance with children’s online privacy protection Act

Protecting the privacy of the young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information on our website from those we know are under 13 years old, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13

Changes To The Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your personal information.
When we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page, and your continued use of our website following the posting of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, or if you wish to exercise your rights regarding your personal information, please contact us at the email address provided in the "Contact Us" section of our website.
